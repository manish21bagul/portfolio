About Me

Know Me More

Hi, My name is Manish Bagul

I'm a designer based in Mumbai. Currently, I work for Cybertech Systems & Software Ltd. in Thane. I've been passionate about design for as long as I can remember, and I have 13 years of experience in user interfaces, graphic design, and front-end development.

My curiosity drives me to explore and learn new things, which is also reflected in my work as a designer. I consider myself a idealist, but I also enjoy exploring new areas of design. My approach to design is guided by a balance between divergent and convergent thinking, artistic flair, and a scientific mindset, creative chaos, and a focus on details.

I have worked at Cybertech for 11 years, where I have learned to manage UI component libraries, create responsive websites, and develop UI assets. Additionally, I have completed a Udemy course online, which has helped me to improve my UI design skills by working on various projects, including iOS applications, web apps, responsive websites, and adding features to existing platforms.

I have extensive experience working remotely, handling complex projects, and meeting tight deadlines. During my career, I have learned how to collaborate effectively with cross-functional partners, including product managers and engineers. I combine my versatile experience with my ability to connect with people to create the best work environment with my team.
